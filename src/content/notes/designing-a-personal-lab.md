## Context

Hemisphere is being rebuilt as a current working surface.

## Decision

Treat the site as a personal creative engineering lab rather than a profile or label front page.

## Reasoning

A personal site can behave less like a profile and more like an instrument panel for active thinking.

## Implementation

Keep the surface compact, static, legible, and able to point toward systems that are still forming.

## Result

The homepage can stay focused on current practice while older work moves into the archive layer.

## Next

Continue shaping the public routes around projects, systems, notes, archive, and contact.
